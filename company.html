Organization

Company

VitalBee is developed by Team DİH—three final-year Computer Engineering students at Istanbul Technical University (ITU).

How we are built

The team is organized around the full stack of the product: system architecture and integration, AI and onboard inference, and signal processing for detection in noisy, debris-heavy conditions. Everyone owns a slice of the stack end to end.

How we work

We have delivered multiple engineering projects together before, so communication, milestones, and technical debt are managed with the same rhythm. The roadmap prioritizes a field-ready prototype, then pilots with municipalities, civil protection, and humanitarian partners.

Partners & trust

Collaboration with industry and research partners is part of the plan for sensors, edge AI, and explainable outputs that rescue teams can trust under pressure. Meet the individuals on the Team page.
